WHO WE ARE

The project team “Aqua Wizards” is a professional website development team founded by a group of students who are motivated to create innovative technology products to deal with issues of water injury and committed to reducing surfing injury or accident in Australia per year.

What We Do

Our project attempts to recommend the safest beach to Australian surfers by inculcating unique features such as shark attack prediction, recommend the best emergency department of the surfing area and insights about the potentially favourable environment for better surfing experience.

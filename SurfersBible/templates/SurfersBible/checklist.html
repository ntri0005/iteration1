What equipments do you need to start your journey?

Surfing Board

Take on the waves without getting wiped out.

Surfing Leash

Surf leashes may save your life. You're tired and getting pounded.

Surfing Fans

Surfing fans provides you stability, control and direction on your surfboard.

Wetsuit

A wetsuit is a garment worn to provide thermal protection while wet.

Surfing Shoes

Surfing shoes are flexible and made with mesh material for enhanced breathability.

Surfing Gloves

Surf gloves will keep your paddling warms ready for the next freezing wave.

Surf Earplugs

Wearing earplugs which can reduce cold water flushing in your ear canal enough to allow the water in there to stay at body temperature.

Surfboard wax

Surfboard wax provides grip and traction for your feet on your surfboard.
